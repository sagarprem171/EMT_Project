About WorkNest

WorkNest is a modern employee management solution crafted for small and growing organizations. Our mission is to provide easy-to-use, affordable, and powerful software that simplifies the way teams are managed.

Designed with flexibility in mind, WorkNest adapts to the unique workflows of different industries — whether it's verifying staff in a school, managing shifts in a store, or rolling out payroll in a restaurant.

Our Values: ✅ Simplicity   ✅ Security   ✅ Customizability   ✅ Dedicated Support

What WorkNest Offers

  • 👥 Employee Registration & Onboarding – Allow new staff to register and upload identity documents.
  • 🛂 Admin Review & Approval – Secure panel for reviewing and approving employee profiles.
  • 📝 Staff Directory Management – Easily update or delete employee records, assign roles, and monitor status.
  • 📄 Payroll Generation – Automatically generate monthly salary reports and individual payslips.
  • 🔐 Employee Self-Login – Let staff securely log in to access their payslips and profile.
  • 📆 Reminder & Notification System – Set organization-specific alerts and task reminders.
  • 📊 Reporting & Analytics – Quick insights into workforce size, payroll history, and activity logs.
